Server Rules

To ensure a safe and positive environment, please follow these rules.

1

Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

2

No Spamming

Avoid sending repetitive messages, images, or links. This includes excessive use of mentions or caps.

3

Keep it SFW

All content shared must be Safe For Work (SFW). No explicit, graphic, or inappropriate material is allowed.

4

Follow Discord's ToS

All members are expected to adhere to Discord's official Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
